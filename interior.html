Modular Kitchen

Kitchen design concept that uses pre-made cabinet modules or units for optimal organization and functionality. These units are crafted from durable materials like plywood, MDF, or particleboard.

A modular kitchen is designed to make cooking and organizing easy and efficient. With designated spaces for appliances, utensils, and groceries, it helps keep the kitchen clutter-free and accessible.

Bedroom Cupboard

Storage unit designed to organize and store clothes, accessories, and other personal items. Made from durable materials like plywood, laminates, or MDF.

A bedroom cupboard is essential for keeping your space tidy and organized. It provides ample storage for clothing, shoes, and accessories.

TV Unit

Stylish and functional piece of furniture designed to hold and organize your television, entertainment devices, and accessories.

A TV unit serves as the centerpiece of your living or entertainment area, offering storage for media devices, gaming consoles, speakers, and accessories like remote controls and DVDs.

False Ceiling

A false ceiling, also known as a drop ceiling or suspended ceiling, is a secondary ceiling installed below the main ceiling. Made from materials like gypsum, plasterboard, or POP (Plaster of Paris).

False ceilings serve multiple purposes in both residential and commercial spaces. They help conceal electrical wiring, ductwork, and other utilities for a clean and polished look.

Curtains

Curtains are fabric panels used to cover windows, adding style, privacy, and light control to a room. They come in various materials, patterns, and designs to suit different interior themes.

Curtains are essential for both residential and commercial spaces. They regulate natural light, helping to create a comfortable ambiance while protecting furniture from direct sunlight.

Flooring

A permanent covering of a floor, designed to provide durability, functionality, and aesthetic appeal to a space. It is available in various materials such as wood, laminate, vinyl, tiles, and natural stone.

Flooring plays a crucial role in enhancing the look and functionality of a space. It provides a stable surface for walking and furniture placement while contributing to the overall interior design.
